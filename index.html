Welcome to my project! :-)

Please feel free to use the dropdown menu below on the left-most column of this page. The dropdown allows the user [you] to select an ID Number of a person who volunteered to have samples collected from their naval (belly-button). Upon doing so, this dynamically generates/displays (1) demographics of said case-study volunteer, (2) the frequency in which the volunteer washes their belly-button throughout the week (displayed in a gauge chart in the center of the page), (3) a bar chart on the right-most column of the page which displays all of the bacterial specimen(s) found inside of the volunteer's bellybutton, and (4) a bubble chart at the bottom of the page which displays Bacteria Cultures Per Sample.

All of the information displayed on this page comes from a .json file. (Please click here if you would like to view the .json file)
